What is a Developer?


According to BLS.gov , a Software Developer is the creative mind behind computer programs. In layman’s terms this means that a Developer takes and idea that people have and turns into a program that can be run and provide some sort of service to its users. This could be an application to make work productivity better, it could be a website, or it could be a game for your phone. Being a software developer, you can decide how to go about presenting the idea in a way to maximize its usefulness, the only limits are what you know and how willing you are to expand your knowledge.
